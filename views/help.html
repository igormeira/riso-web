First Step: Search for a term.

  • To search a term that has not a context yet, just type the term.

    Example: jaguar

  • To search for a term that already has a context, type the term + underscore + context.

    Example: jaguar_car

Second Step: Choose which contexts you'd like.

  • Check the context you'd like to apply to your term.
  • After check it. Press the search icon to enrich your term.

Third Step: Enrich your search.

  • Select the desired terms.
  • Click on terms to be refined.
  • Click on "indexed" tab to see the results.

Optional Step: See your tree.

  • Click on "Tree" tab to see the result tree about your search.

RISO - Indexing And Retrieval Of Semantic Information Of Text Objects

This project aims to create an indexing and semantic retrieval environment. Documents providing a more accurate recovery, improving the accuracy of results by reducing the ambiguity of the meaning of the terms. RISO is separated into three parts:

  • RISO-DV (Domain Vocabularies)
  • RISO-SI (Semantic Indexing)
  • RISO-SR (Semantic Retrieval)
